Biography

I am a 2nd-year Master student from department of Computer Science in Nanjing University , under the supervision of A.P. Wei Wang . My research interests mainly include Wireless Sensing and Mobile Computing. Now I am focusing on the novel Human Computer Interaction system based on acoustic signal.

I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics in 2016. During my undergraduate years, I received the CCF(China Computer Federation) Outstanding Undergraduate Award. I also achieved gold medal the 40th ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest (ACM/ICPC) Asia Regional Contest.
