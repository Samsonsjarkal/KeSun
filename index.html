Biography

I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics in 2016. During my undergraduate years, I participated in the ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest (ACM/ICPC) and received gold medal in 40th ACM-ICPC Asian Regional Contest. Currently I am a 2nd-year Master student from department of Computer Science in Nanjing University, under the supervision of A.P. Wei Wang .
My research interests mainly include Wireless Sensing and Mobile Computing. Now I focus on the novel Human Computer Interaction system based on acoustic signal.
